How to Create a QR Code

Enter your URL or text in the input field above. Optionally choose a custom QR color and background color, and upload your logo. Click Generate QR Code and your QR code appears instantly. Click Download to save it as a PNG image file ready to use anywhere.

What Can You Put in a QR Code?

  • Website URLs — link to your website, blog, or online store
  • Social media profiles — YouTube, Instagram, Telegram, Facebook pages
  • Plain text — messages, contact info, addresses
  • Phone numbers — scan to call directly
  • Email addresses — scan to open email client
  • WiFi credentials — share WiFi passwords without typing
  • Product pages — link physical products to online listings

Will My QR Code With a Logo Still Scan?

Yes. QR codes use built-in error correction technology. This generator uses the highest error correction level (H), which means up to 30% of the QR code can be covered by a logo and the code will still scan perfectly on any smartphone camera.

How to Use QR Codes Effectively

For YouTube creators

Create a QR code linking to your YouTube channel and add it to your thumbnails, end screens, or printed merchandise. Viewers can scan and subscribe instantly.

For Telegram channels

Generate a QR code with your Telegram invite link. Print it on flyers or share in images to grow your channel faster.

For businesses

Add QR codes to business cards, packaging, menus, or posters to link customers directly to your website or social media.

No. QR codes generated here are static and never expire. As long as the URL or content you entered remains valid, the QR code will work forever.
