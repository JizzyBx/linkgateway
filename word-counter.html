How to Use This Word Counter

Simply type or paste your text into the text area above. The word counter updates instantly in real time — no button clicks needed. You can see your word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, estimated read time, and social media character limit progress all at once.

Why Use a Word Counter?

  • Essays and assignments — stay within word count requirements for school or university work
  • Social media posts — check against Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook character limits before posting
  • Blog articles — track article length and estimate how long it takes to read
  • SEO content — Google tends to rank longer, more comprehensive content higher in search results
  • Copywriting — stay within ad copy character limits for Google Ads and Facebook Ads

What the Word Counter Measures

Words

Counts the total number of words by splitting text on whitespace. Multiple spaces between words are handled correctly and do not inflate the count.

Characters

Shows total characters including spaces, and also characters without spaces separately. Both counts are useful depending on your platform's requirements.

Sentences

Counts sentence-ending punctuation marks (periods, exclamation marks, question marks) to estimate the number of sentences in your text.

Read Time

Estimated based on the average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute. A 1000-word article takes approximately 5 minutes to read.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this count words in real time?

Yes. The counter updates instantly with every keystroke as you type or edit your text. No need to click any button.

What is the Twitter character limit?

Twitter (X) allows 280 characters per tweet. The live bar above shows exactly how close you are to the limit as you type.

Can I count words in a PDF or Word document?

Simply copy the text from your PDF or Word document and paste it into the text area. The counter works with any text content.
