How to Use the Age Calculator

Using this free age calculator is simple. Select your date of birth from the date picker, choose the date you want to calculate your age as of (defaults to today), and click Calculate My Age. Your exact age in years, months, and days appears instantly along with bonus stats.

What This Age Calculator Shows You

  • Total days and weeks you have been alive
  • Total minutes lived — a surprisingly large number!
  • Next birthday countdown — days until your next celebration

How Old Am I? Common Questions

How is age calculated exactly?

Age is calculated by finding the difference between your date of birth and today's date. The calculator accounts for leap years, different month lengths, and partial months to give you a precise result down to the exact day.

Can I calculate age between two specific dates?

Yes. Simply change the "Calculate Age As Of" date to any date in the past or future. This is useful for calculating age at a specific event, historical date, or future milestone.

Why do different calculators give different results?

Some calculators round to the nearest year while others calculate exact years, months, and days. Our calculator uses precise date arithmetic to give you the most accurate result possible.
